Practice Architecture Programme

Structural clarity for advice firms that intend to scale deliberately. We align segmentation, capacity and service standards so operational strain reduces and commercial confidence increases.

View Programme Brochure Review & Sign Terms of Engagement

Why This Exists

Growth without design creates friction. Friction reduces capacity. Reduced capacity forces reactive decisions. The Practice Architecture Programme is built to restore clarity before scale.
  • Clear segmentation logic that aligns with commercial reality
  • Controlled capacity through defined roles, ownership and workflow
  • Service standards that remain consistent across advisers
  • Reduced operational strain and fewer bottlenecks
  • A deliberate roadmap for expansion, hiring and intake discipline
  • Leadership confidence based on structure, not gut feel

The Programme

Delivered in three phases. Each phase is optional. There is no obligation to proceed beyond Phase I.

Phase I – Strategic Diagnostic

Establish structural clarity by examining segmentation, capacity distortion and operational friction.

  • Operating model snapshot
  • Task allocation and role clarity review
  • Revenue distribution and service alignment
  • Diagnostic report with key findings

£3,000

No obligation beyond Phase I.

Phase II – Structural Design

Design the architecture required for deliberate scale, based on Phase I findings.

  • Segmentation redesign
  • Service standards and cadence
  • Capacity and workflow design
  • Defined intake criteria

From £6,000

Subject to scope confirmation and illustrative proposal.

Phase III – Operational Activation

Embed the design into day-to-day operations with clarity, accountability and rhythm.

  • Implementation support
  • Team alignment and adoption
  • SOP and workflow activation
  • Operating cadence and review points

From £6,000

Subject to scope confirmation and illustrative proposal.

Payment structure: 50% payable upfront per phase, with the remaining 50% payable prior to delivery of that phase’s outputs.

Structural Clarity Creates Strategic Freedom

  • Controlled Capacity – predictable adviser workload and clearer ownership
  • Revenue Discipline – service intensity aligned to client value
  • Scalable Growth – defined intake criteria and expansion readiness
  • Operational Stability – less firefighting, fewer bottlenecks
  • Leadership Confidence – decisions based on structure, not pressure
  • Consistency – reduced variation in servicing across advisers
Clarity reduces friction. Friction reduction creates momentum.
