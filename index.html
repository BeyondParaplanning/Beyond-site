About Beyond

A Message from Our Founder

Since the very start of my working life — both inside and outside of financial services — I've always found myself drawn to improving how things run. Whether I was piloting new processes or streamlining systems, I kept ending up in roles where the goal was the same: make things smoother, simpler, and more effective. And the truth is, I've found real joy in it.

That passion eventually led me to create Beyond.

After years of supporting advisers in-house, I saw how much time and energy gets lost to reporting, research, and admin — time that could be spent on the work that really matters. So I set out to offer something different: intelligent, reliable support that gives advisers space to do what only they can do.

Whether it's suitability and review reports, fund research, tool building or strategic operational help, Beyond is here to take the weight off your shoulders — so you can focus on your clients, your business, and the bigger picture.

We don't just deliver outputs — we give you back your time, energy, and headspace.

Save time. Save energy. Go Beyond.

Thanks for being here — I'd love to hear how we can support your journey.

Warmly,

Report Writing Services

FCA-compliant reports tailored to your process. Delivered reliably, with no corner-cutting.

Transparent, modular pricing — designed for clarity and fairness

  • Annual Review Report – typically £100–£150
  • Protection Suitability Report – typically £150–£200
  • Mortgage Suitability Report – typically £100–£150
  • Investment or Pension Suitability Report – typically £300–£400
  • Multi-area or joint cases – usually £450–£600
  • Drawdown, cashflow or complex planning – £600–£900+

  • Additional research – £60 per hour (after first hour)

Our Process

Working with Beyond is straightforward and designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing business processes. Here's how we work together:

Initial Consultation

We start by understanding your business, your advice process, and your specific needs. This helps us tailor our approach to complement your existing systems.

Onboarding

We'll set up secure channels for information sharing and establish clear communication protocols, ensuring a smooth working relationship from day one.

Task Submission

When you need our support, simply submit your request along with the relevant client information via secure messaging.

Quality Work Delivered
