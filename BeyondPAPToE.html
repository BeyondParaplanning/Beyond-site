Terms of Engagement

Practice Architecture Programme

Effective Date: 1 February 2026

How this document works: These Terms govern the Practice Architecture Programme as a whole (Phases I–III). Only Phase I is accepted and initiated on signature. Progression to Phase II and Phase III is optional and requires separate written acceptance.

These Terms of Engagement are entered into between Beyond Intelligent Adviser Solutions (“Beyond”, “we”, “us”) and the Client (the firm signing below).

Programme terms (Phases I–III) Phase-by-phase opt-in 7-day cooling-off per phase 50% upfront, 50% prior to delivery Liability capped per phase

1. Programme Structure

The Programme is delivered in defined phases:

  • Phase I – Strategic Diagnostic
  • Phase II – Structural Design
  • Phase III – Operational Activation

Each phase is a distinct layer of work. Progression between phases is optional and requires written agreement. There is no obligation to proceed beyond any completed phase.

2. Nature of Services

Beyond provides strategic, architectural advisory services. Services do not constitute regulated financial advice, compliance certification, or operational management. Ultimate responsibility for business decisions and implementation rests solely with the Client’s directors.

3. Phase Scopes (Summary)

Detailed scope and outputs for Phase II and Phase III will be confirmed in the illustrative proposal for that phase.

Phase I – Strategic Diagnostic

  • Segmentation logic and service alignment review
  • Capacity and task distribution analysis
  • Operational workflow observation and friction points
  • Revenue distribution and structural strain indicators

Outputs: written Diagnostic Report and defined structural observations.

Phase I does not include implementation, structural redesign, regulated advice, or compliance sign-off.

Phase II – Structural Design

  • Segmentation model design and thresholds
  • Service standards, cadence and access rules
  • Capacity model and role ownership
  • Defined intake criteria and operating architecture

Outputs to be confirmed in the Phase II illustrative proposal following Phase I.

Phase III – Operational Activation

  • Activation support to embed the design into operations
  • Team alignment and adoption rhythm
  • SOP and workflow activation (where in scope)
  • Operating cadence and review checkpoints

Outputs to be confirmed in the Phase III illustrative proposal following Phase I.

5. Cooling-Off Period (Applies Per Phase)

A 7-day cooling-off period applies to each phase from the date of written acceptance of that phase.

The initial 50% phase fee is payable upon acceptance. No substantive work will commence until the expiry of the cooling-off period.

If the Client provides written notice of cancellation within the cooling-off period, any payment made for that phase will be refunded in full.

Following expiry of the cooling-off period, the relevant phase is deemed formally commenced and all fees paid become non-refundable.

6. Data and Access

The Client agrees to provide accurate and timely information necessary for architectural examination and to facilitate access to relevant personnel. Findings and recommendations are dependent upon the quality and completeness of information supplied.

7. Confidentiality

All information shared within the Programme will be treated as confidential and used solely for the purposes of delivering the agreed phase. Beyond will not disclose confidential information to third parties without written consent, except where legally required.

8. Intellectual Property

All architectural methodologies, frameworks, models and tools remain the intellectual property of Beyond unless otherwise agreed in writing. The Client is granted internal usage rights for delivered outputs within their firm.

9. Advisory Nature and Reliance

Recommendations are non-binding and provided for strategic guidance. Beyond does not assume operational control of the Client’s business. Responsibility for decisions taken and any implementation remains with the Client’s directors.

10. Limitation of Liability

Beyond’s total aggregate liability arising from any individual phase shall be limited to the total fee paid for that phase. Beyond shall not be liable for indirect, consequential, or commercial losses arising from implementation decisions taken by the Client.

11. Termination

Either party may terminate prior to commencement of a phase in writing. Following commencement of a phase, fees paid are non-refundable. Fees remain payable for work completed up to the termination date.

12. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.
