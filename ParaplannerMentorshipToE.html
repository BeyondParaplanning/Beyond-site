Terms of Engagement

Trainee Paraplanner Technical Support

Effective Date: 17 February 2026

How this document works: These Terms govern a monthly retainer arrangement for trainee/junior paraplanner technical support (primarily via consolidated email queries and scheduled Teams discussions). The monthly fee is based on the selected weekly allocation (£250 per month per allocated hour per week).

These Terms of Engagement are entered into between Beyond (“Beyond”, “we”, “us”) and the Client (the firm signing below).

Retainer-based mentorship Scheduled support blocks Consolidated email queries Developmental guidance Not regulated advice

1. Purpose

The purpose of this arrangement is to support the development of a trainee/junior paraplanner through structured technical guidance. The objective is to increase competence, confidence, and independence by improving reasoning, report structure, research method and clarity.

2. Nature of Services

Beyond provides technical guidance and mentoring support. This service does not constitute regulated financial advice, compliance certification, supervision under the firm’s regulatory permissions, or responsibility for advice suitability.

The Client retains full responsibility for all client recommendations, compliance sign-off, supervision and regulatory oversight.

3. Scope of Support

The retainer may include, within the agreed time allocation:

  • Review and response to consolidated technical queries submitted by email
  • Scheduled Teams calls to discuss cases, reasoning, report structure and approach
  • Feedback on draft paraplanning outputs (for example: suitability letter structure, research approach, charge presentation)
  • Guidance on presenting pros/cons, risk language, assumptions, and recommendation rationale
  • Development support and pattern-based learning to reduce reliance over time

This service is designed to support development and decision-making quality, not to act as an “always-on” helpdesk. Where the nature of support shifts into operational delivery (for example: producing reports), this will be treated as separate paraplanning work under separate terms.

4. Support Method and Turnaround

Support is delivered primarily via email and scheduled Teams blocks. To maintain clarity and avoid delays:

  • Queries should be consolidated where possible (rather than sent as multiple small emails).
  • Beyond will typically respond within 1–2 working days (subject to volume and agreed allocation).
  • Where a query is complex, Beyond may suggest it is best handled in the next scheduled Teams session.

Urgent client matters and regulatory deadlines remain the responsibility of the Client and supervising adviser(s).

5. Fees and Allocation

The monthly retainer fee is based on the selected weekly allocation: £250 per month per allocated hour per week.

Fee Basis £250 per month per allocated hour per week
Example 1 hour per week equivalent = £250 per month
Payment Monthly in advance. Due within 7 days of invoice.

Beyond is not currently VAT registered. If VAT registration status changes, fees will be adjusted accordingly.

Additional time: If support requirements consistently exceed the agreed allocation, we will either (a) agree an increased retainer, or (b) invoice additional time separately at £60 per hour, by agreement in advance.

6. Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to:

  • Ensure the trainee/junior paraplanner operates under appropriate internal supervision
  • Provide timely and accurate information required for case discussion
  • Ensure any client information shared is limited to what is necessary and redacted where appropriate
  • Retain responsibility for all advice decisions and compliance sign-off

7. Confidentiality and Data Protection

Both parties will treat information shared as confidential and will not disclose it to third parties without consent, except where legally required. The Client remains responsible for ensuring appropriate permissions, lawful basis, and secure transfer for any client information shared.

8. Intellectual Property

Any frameworks, templates, methodologies, or written guidance created by Beyond remain the intellectual property of Beyond unless otherwise agreed in writing. The Client is granted internal usage rights for training and internal development purposes only.

9. Limitation of Liability

Beyond provides guidance only. The Client retains responsibility for decisions taken and any implementation. Beyond shall not be liable for any loss arising from advice issued by the Client, compliance breaches, or regulatory complaints.

Beyond’s total aggregate liability under these Terms shall be limited to the total retainer fees paid by the Client in the 3 months preceding any claim.

10. Term, Review and Termination

This agreement operates on a rolling monthly basis unless otherwise agreed in writing. Either party may terminate the agreement by providing 30 days’ written notice.

If termination occurs, fees already paid for the current month remain payable/non-refundable and services will continue until the end of the notice period unless otherwise agreed.

11. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.
