Terms of Business

Mentoring, Technical Coaching & Professional Development Services

Service Provider: Beyond Paraplanning & Intelligent Adviser Solutions

1. Introduction

These Terms of Business set out the basis upon which Beyond Paraplanning & Intelligent Adviser Solutions ("Beyond Paraplanning", "we", "us", "our") will provide mentoring, technical coaching and professional development services to the Client named in the Engagement Details section of this document ("the Client").

By engaging our services, the Client agrees to be bound by these Terms of Business.

2. Services

Beyond Paraplanning provides mentoring, technical coaching and professional development services to financial services professionals and businesses.

The purpose of the engagement is to support the Client's learning, technical understanding, professional development and business growth through mentoring discussions, knowledge sharing, guidance and challenge.

Services may include, but are not limited to:

  • Technical mentoring and coaching
  • Case discussion and learning support
  • Guidance on research approaches and information sources
  • Professional development support
  • Examination and qualification guidance
  • Industry knowledge sharing
  • Business development discussions
  • Operational guidance
  • Practice management discussions
  • Training sessions
  • Workshops

The precise scope of support will be determined by the Client's requirements and may evolve throughout the engagement.

Any outsourced paraplanning, report writing, research, technical analysis, file checking, suitability assessment or other paraplanning-related services provided by Beyond Paraplanning shall be subject to a separate agreement and separate Terms of Engagement.

Nothing within these Terms of Business shall oblige Beyond Paraplanning to provide such services, nor shall the provision of mentoring services under this agreement be construed as the provision of outsourced paraplanning services.

3. Nature of the Relationship

The purpose of the engagement is to provide guidance, support, knowledge sharing and professional insight.

The Client acknowledges that Beyond Paraplanning is not acting as the Client's compliance consultant, file checker, regulated adviser or appointed representative under this agreement.

The Client remains solely responsible for:

  • Advice provided to end clients
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Suitability of recommendations
  • Research conclusions
  • Client communications
  • File quality
  • Obtaining any required compliance approval
  • Decisions made by the Client or their business

No Regulated Advice

The services provided under this agreement are educational, consultative and developmental in nature.

Beyond Paraplanning does not provide regulated financial advice to the Client or the Client's customers under this agreement.

The Client remains solely responsible for determining the suitability and appropriateness of any recommendation, action or course of conduct.

4. Mentor, Not Checker or Delivery Service

Beyond Paraplanning provides mentoring, coaching and guidance only under this agreement.

Beyond Paraplanning may discuss technical matters, research approaches, information sources, regulatory considerations and potential areas for further investigation.

However, Beyond Paraplanning does not undertake responsibility for conducting research, producing recommendations, drafting reports, assessing suitability, checking files, providing compliance approval or delivering regulated advice under this agreement.

Responsibility for all research, analysis, recommendations, suitability assessments, reports and client communications remains with the Client.

Any paraplanning or technical work subsequently undertaken by Beyond Paraplanning shall be governed by a separate agreement and shall not fall within the scope of these Terms of Business.

5. Independent Contractor

Nothing in this agreement shall create a partnership, joint venture, agency relationship or contract of employment between the parties.

Beyond Paraplanning shall provide the services as an independent contractor at all times.

6. Delivery of Services

Support may be delivered through a combination of:

  • Microsoft Teams meetings
  • Telephone calls
  • Email correspondence
  • In-person meetings
  • Training sessions
  • Workshops
  • Written feedback
  • Learning discussions

The method of delivery will be agreed between both parties according to the nature of the support required.

Beyond Paraplanning will use reasonable endeavours to respond to requests in a timely manner but does not guarantee availability within a specific timeframe unless otherwise agreed.

7. Fees

Services shall be charged at the rate agreed between the parties from time to time.

Beyond Paraplanning reserves the right to review and amend its fees periodically.

Any change in fees will be communicated to the Client with a minimum of 30 days' written notice and shall only apply to work undertaken after the revised fee has taken effect.

Chargeable time may include:

  • Mentoring sessions
  • Technical discussions
  • Case discussions for learning purposes
  • Training sessions
  • Business development discussions
  • Telephone calls
  • Video meetings
  • Email correspondence
  • Preparation time
  • Follow-up actions

Time will be recorded in 15-minute increments and rounded up to the nearest 15 minutes where applicable.

8. Travel and Expenses

Where support is provided at the Client's premises or another agreed location:

  • Mileage will be charged at the prevailing rate advised to the Client from time to time
  • Parking charges and other reasonable travel expenses may also be recharged where applicable
  • Significant travel costs will be discussed and agreed in advance where reasonably possible

9. Invoicing and Payment

Invoices will be issued monthly in arrears.

Payment is due within 7 days of the invoice date unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Payments should be made by bank transfer to the account specified on the invoice.

Beyond Paraplanning reserves the right to suspend services where invoices remain unpaid beyond the due date.

Interest may be charged on overdue amounts in accordance with the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998.

10. Right to Refuse Work

Beyond Paraplanning reserves the right to decline any request for support where:

  • The request falls outside the scope of this agreement
  • The request may create a conflict of interest
  • The request may breach legal, regulatory or professional obligations
  • Sufficient capacity is not available

Where reasonably possible, the Client will be informed of the reasons for any such refusal.

11. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to treat all confidential information disclosed during the course of the engagement as confidential and not disclose such information to any third party unless:

  • Required by law or regulation
  • Disclosure is authorised by the other party
  • The information is already in the public domain through no fault of the receiving party

This obligation shall continue after termination of the agreement.

12. Data Protection

Both parties agree to comply with applicable UK data protection legislation, including the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Where client cases are discussed, the Client remains responsible for ensuring they have the appropriate authority and lawful basis to share information.

Where reasonably possible, personal data should be anonymised before being shared.

13. Intellectual Property

Any templates, frameworks, checklists, processes, methodologies, training materials, guides, resources or documentation supplied by Beyond Paraplanning shall remain the intellectual property of Beyond Paraplanning unless otherwise agreed in writing.

The Client is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use such materials solely for its own internal business purposes.

The Client may not reproduce, distribute, licence, sell or otherwise make such materials available to third parties without prior written consent.

14. No Guarantees

Whilst Beyond Paraplanning will exercise reasonable skill, care and diligence in providing services, no guarantee is given regarding:

  • Examination results
  • Professional qualifications
  • Business growth
  • Revenue generation
  • Regulatory approval
  • Client acquisition
  • Specific technical outcomes
  • Commercial success

The Client acknowledges that outcomes depend upon factors outside the control of Beyond Paraplanning.

15. Limitation of Liability

Nothing within these Terms of Business shall limit liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, fraud, fraudulent misrepresentation or any liability that cannot lawfully be excluded.

Subject to the above, Beyond Paraplanning's total aggregate liability arising from the provision of services shall not exceed the greater of:

  • The total fees paid by the Client during the three months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim; or
  • £1,000.

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Beyond Paraplanning shall not be liable for any indirect, consequential or special loss, including loss of profit, loss of revenue, loss of business opportunity or loss of goodwill arising from the provision of services under this agreement.

16. No Third-Party Reliance

Services are provided solely for the benefit of the Client.

No duty of care is owed to any underlying client, customer, employee, contractor or third party.

No third party may rely upon any guidance, comments, opinions, materials or communications provided by Beyond Paraplanning.

17. Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for any delay or failure to perform its obligations under this agreement where such delay or failure arises from circumstances beyond its reasonable control.

18. Term and Termination

This agreement shall commence on the agreed start date and continue on a rolling monthly basis.

Either party may terminate this agreement at any time by providing not less than 14 days' written notice.

Either party may terminate the agreement immediately where the other party commits a material breach of these Terms of Business and fails to remedy that breach within 14 days of being requested to do so.

Any fees incurred and expenses accrued up to the termination date shall remain payable.

Termination shall not affect any rights or obligations accrued prior to termination.

19. Complaints

Should the Client have any concerns regarding the services provided, they should notify Beyond Paraplanning in writing as soon as reasonably practicable.

Both parties agree to attempt to resolve any concerns in good faith before pursuing formal legal action.

20. Entire Agreement

These Terms of Business constitute the entire agreement between the parties and supersede all previous discussions, proposals, understandings and arrangements relating to the services provided.

21. Variations

No variation to these Terms of Business shall be effective unless agreed in writing by both parties.

22. Governing Law

These Terms of Business shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales.

Any dispute arising from or in connection with these Terms of Business shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.
